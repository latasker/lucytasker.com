Lucy Tasker is a clarinetist of Belgian and Welsh heritage who is based between Brussels and London.
She was born in Tokyo, Japan where she lived until she was 15, studying music through the Yamaha music method in Tokyo. 

First and foremost, Lucy is an improviser, a versatile player who enjoys playing in a variety of contexts.
Lucy has the most experience playing swing, contemporary jazz & improvisation, Balkan music, and Nepalese fusion folk.

Lucy is a fluent speaker of English, French, Japanese, and has a rudimentary understanding of Korean and Spanish.
